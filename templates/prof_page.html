About Prof. Shim

Joon Sub Shim received his B.Sc. degree in both Mechanical engineering and Electrical engineering from KAIST (Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology) in 2004. He attained his Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from University of Cincinnati. During his Ph. D. study, he joined the BioMEMS lab and has done graduate research under the research guidance of Prof. Chong H. Ahn. He worked at Samsung Advanced Institute of Technology (SAIT) with a professional research work to develop DNA analyzer by micro/nanotechnology. He is now working at Kwangwoon University as an associate professor and researching about the next-generation nanotechnology for bio and energy applications.
